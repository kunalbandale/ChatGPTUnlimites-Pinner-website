Privacy FAQ

Do you collect any personal information?
No, we do not collect any personal information. The extension only stores the titles and URLs of conversations you choose to pin, and this data stays on your device only.
Can you see my ChatGPT conversations?
No, we cannot see the content of your ChatGPT conversations. We only store the titles and URLs of conversations you pin, not the actual conversation content.
Is my data shared with third parties?
No, we do not share any data with third parties. Since we don't collect any data in the first place, there's nothing to share.
What happens to my data if I uninstall the extension?
When you uninstall the extension, all stored data (pinned conversation titles and URLs) is automatically deleted from your device. No traces remain.
Does the extension work offline?
Yes, once installed, the extension works completely offline. It doesn't require an internet connection to function.
