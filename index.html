Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our extension.

Is this extension really free?
Yes! Our extension is completely free to use with no hidden costs, premium features, or subscriptions.
Does it work with all ChatGPT conversations?
Yes, you can pin any conversation in ChatGPT, including both new and existing chats.
Will my pinned conversations sync across devices?
Yes, if you're logged into the same Chrome account on multiple devices, your pinned conversations will sync automatically.
What happens if I uninstall the extension?
Your pinned conversations will be removed from the sidebar, but your actual ChatGPT conversations remain untouched.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely! We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything is stored locally on your device only.
